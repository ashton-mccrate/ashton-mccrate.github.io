Ashton McCrate is a writer living in Portland, OR. Her work can be found in Portland Zine Symposium's Mistakes - Anthology, in Scattering Theory, an anthology from IPRC's 2020-2021 "Write a Book" workshop, and in her self published poetry chapbook, Rudimentary Eyes.

Her work has been described as "domestic and estranged" with "one foot in the absurd and the other in the banal of the real". Common themes in her work include: family, identity, mental illness, and (sometimes pseudo) science.
